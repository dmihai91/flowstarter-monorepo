About the stylist

Beauty direction with an editorial eye

Studio Aria was built around a simple idea: luxury should feel intentional, not intimidating. Every appointment is shaped around the client in the chair, not a fixed salon script.

After training in Paris and New York, Sophia developed a practice that merges fashion-set polish with real-life wearability. The result is beauty work that photographs beautifully, grows out gracefully, and still feels like you.

Trained across advanced color, cutting, and editorial makeup programs
Trusted for bridal styling, beauty campaigns, and VIP event prep
Known for low-stress appointments with high-detail finishing
Featured by clients who want polish without over-styling

Consultation-led

Every transformation starts with face shape, lifestyle, texture history, and the visual tone you want to carry.

Refined finish

The focus is shine, movement, and thoughtful detailing rather than heavy-handed trends that date quickly.

Photo-aware

Looks are built to read beautifully in person and on camera, whether the setting is a wedding aisle or a campaign set.

Luxury pace

Private sessions create room for care, conversation, and a calmer experience from arrival to final reveal.
