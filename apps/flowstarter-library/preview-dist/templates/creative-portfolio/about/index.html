About the studio

Strategy first. Visuals with tension.

Marcus Rivera Creative sits at the intersection of design thinking and editorial image-making. Projects begin with narrative, positioning, and audience behavior, then move toward visuals that carry that strategy without feeling overexplained.

Based in Brooklyn, Marcus works across identity, web, photography, and campaign direction for founders, cultural institutions, and product teams that want sharper visual language. The practice values contrast, restraint, and a strong editorial eye over trend-chasing.

Independent creative director partnering with startups and cultural brands
Experienced across identity, photography, web, and campaign systems
Known for visual clarity paired with commercial relevance
Selected work recognized by editorial and founder-led teams alike

Narrative-led

Projects begin with what the audience should feel, understand, and remember before any visual treatment is chosen.

Cross-medium

Identity, stills, motion, and digital surfaces are considered together so the final work does not fragment across channels.

Lean collaboration

Small, direct project teams keep communication tight and creative decisions easier to move on.

Distinctive output

The goal is not generic polish. It is work that feels precise, contemporary, and impossible to confuse with someone else's.
