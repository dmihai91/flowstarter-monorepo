FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about working together.

Timelines vary by scope. Brand identity projects typically take 4-6 weeks, web design 6-8 weeks, and photography projects 1-2 weeks from shoot to delivery. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our discovery call.

All projects include 2 rounds of revisions at each milestone. I find that our thorough discovery process means we usually nail it by the first revision. Additional rounds can be added at a reasonable rate.

You do. Upon final payment, all rights to the created work transfer to you. I retain the right to showcase the work in my portfolio unless we agree otherwise.

Projects range from $500 for a single photo session to $15,000+ for comprehensive brand identity packages. I'm transparent about pricing from our first conversation and always work within your budget.

I keep communication open and regular. You'll have access to a shared project board, receive weekly updates, and we'll have check-in calls at each milestone. I'm responsive on email and Slack.
