About

Meet David Chen

David Chen is a Berklee College of Music graduate with 15 years of teaching experience, helping students build technique, confidence, and a lasting connection to music.

David has spent the past 15 years teaching students of all ages, from first-time beginners to working performers preparing for auditions and recordings.

His lessons combine strong technical fundamentals with practical musicianship, so students develop both skill and artistic confidence.

Whether the goal is joyful weekly lessons, exam preparation, or stage-ready performance, David builds a plan that keeps students progressing.
