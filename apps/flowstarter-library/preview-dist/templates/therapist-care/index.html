About Me

A Compassionate Partner in Your Healing Journey

For over 15 years, I've had the privilege of walking alongside individuals, couples, and families as they navigate life's most challenging moments.

I specialize in anxiety, depression, relationship issues, trauma, and life transitions. Whether you're facing a specific challenge or seeking personal growth, I'm here to support you.

My Approach

How We'll Work Together

I believe in a collaborative, client-centered approach that honors your unique experiences and empowers lasting change.

1

Compassionate Listening

I create a safe, non-judgmental space where you can share your thoughts and feelings openly.

2

Evidence-Based Methods

I use proven therapeutic approaches including CBT, EMDR, attachment theory, and mindfulness.

3

Collaborative Process

We work together to set goals and develop strategies that fit your unique needs and values.

4

Holistic Perspective

I consider all aspects of your life—mind, body, relationships—in our work together.

FAQ

Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about therapy with me.

Our first session is about getting to know each other. I'll ask about what brings you to therapy, your history, and your goals. This helps me understand how I can best support you. It's also a chance for you to ask questions and see if we're a good fit.

The length of therapy varies depending on your goals and needs. Some clients find relief in 8-12 sessions, while others benefit from longer-term work. We'll regularly check in on your progress and adjust our approach as needed.

I am an out-of-network provider for most insurance plans. I can provide a superbill for you to submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement. I also offer a sliding scale for those who qualify.

I require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice will be charged the full session fee, except in cases of emergency.

Yes! I offer secure video therapy sessions for clients throughout California. Virtual therapy can be just as effective as in-person sessions and offers added convenience.
